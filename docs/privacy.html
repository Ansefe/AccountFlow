Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use AccountFlow:

  • Account information: email address, display name
  • Payment information: processed directly by Paddle. We do not store credit card data
  • Usage data: tracked game statistics, credit transactions, application activity and performance metrics
  • Technical information: session data for active tracking management

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Process credit transactions and data tracking operations
  • Generate performance analytics and dashboards
  • Maintain the security of the Service
  • Communicate with you about your account or changes to the Service

3. Payment Processing

Payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy, LLC, who acts as the Merchant of Record. Lemon Squeezy collects and processes your payment information according to their own privacy policy. We do not have access to your full credit card details.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase servers (AWS infrastructure) with encryption in transit and at rest. We implement Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure you can only access your own data. User passwords are managed through Supabase's authentication system using bcrypt hashing.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Lemon Squeezy: for payment processing
  • Supabase: as infrastructure provider (data storage)
  • Legal requirements: when required by law

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed within 30 days, except for financial records we must maintain for legal obligations.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

AccountFlow is a desktop application and does not use browser cookies. We use secure authentication tokens (JWT) to manage user sessions.

9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the application or by email.

11. Contact

For privacy inquiries, you may contact us through the application.
