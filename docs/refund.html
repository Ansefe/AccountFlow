Refund Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

1. Subscriptions

Monthly subscriptions may be cancelled at any time. Upon cancellation:

  • You will retain access to the Service until the end of the current billing period
  • No additional charges will be made
  • Unused subscription credits will expire at the end of the period
  • Separately purchased credits will be retained

Subscription refunds may be requested within the first 48 hours after a charge, provided that no credits from the corresponding period have been used.

2. Credit Packages

Credit packages are one-time purchases. Refunds are available under the following conditions:

  • Request made within 24 hours of purchase
  • Credits have not been used (in whole or in part)

No refunds will be issued for credits that have already been consumed.

3. Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues that prevent normal use of the Service (e.g., inability to access tracking features or dashboards), you may request a refund of the affected credits. Each case will be evaluated individually.

4. How to Request a Refund

Refunds are processed by Paddle as Merchant of Record. You may:

  • Contact us directly through the application
  • Use the Paddle customer portal to manage your subscription

Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

5. Exceptions

No refunds will be issued in the following cases:

  • Violation of the Terms of Service
  • Account suspension due to misuse
  • Dissatisfaction with analytics results or data interpretations
